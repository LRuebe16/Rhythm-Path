The Artisans

Moussa

Moussa, a local from Senegal, provides for his family by hand-carving wood to represent numerous West African objet d'art.

Astou

Astou provides for her family by hand-weaving baskets and upcycling materials into wonderful art that is enjoyed on an international level.

Malik

Malick uses his skills to carve out characters in tales of hunting and gathering, kings and queens, and many others.
