How It's Made

Rhythm Path travels to West Africa every other year to learn more about the lifestyles of the locals. The process of building a single drum involves 6-10 different artisans! Our products are authentic West African hand-made instruments that are imported directly from West Africa and help provide jobs for a number of artisans ranging from Ghana to Senegal.
