Artisan Donations

Rhythm Path travels to Malika, Senegal and Accra, Ghana in West Africa to meet each artisan that builds our products so we can teach others about how much work and love goes into everything we sell. Our artisans work day in and day out to provide our customers with authentic instruments that everyone can appreciate. We feel it is important to understand where these instruments come from and the hands that create them.
