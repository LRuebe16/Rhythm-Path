Team Building Events

Rhythm Path is happy to announce a truly unique opportunity for your business to experience collaboration through the cultural exploration of music and rhythms. One of Rhythm Path's facilitators will bring the drums, the fun and the chance to learn and apply team building techniques through music and rhythms. Book Rhythm Path's services today and experience a new path for your next team building event!

School Events - Day of Drumming

Social Emotional Learning while playing drums?! This is a unique assembly program where every student has their very own authentic West African djembe drum to play. Students will work as a team to accomplish the goal of making music together.

Coming Soon - Drum in Class

Rhythm Path is working on a new two week program where students drum together in their music class. The program will be similar to the large “Day of Drumming” assembly program, however, students will have more opportunities in a classroom setting. The program will include drum along video lessons as well as teaching material/plans for the classroom teacher.
